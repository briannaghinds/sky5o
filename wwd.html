What We Do


High speed chases can result in property damage, civilian injuries, and at some times civililan death. If a chase happens, whenever a suspect is fleeing the scene and is said to be a danger to civilians or is a threat to themselves the police will call of the chase, this is known as a "no chase" policy. This is when our technology can be deployed to aid in these situations.

Our program has two modes that offers a range of assistance. Our first mode is manual mode, where the drone can be deployed and controlled via the keyboard. Our second mode is convert license plate, which uses the drone's camera to capture (through a key press) a license plate of the vehicle and transfers the image to a string, for the police to have in their database.
